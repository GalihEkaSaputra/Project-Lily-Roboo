With robotics technology advancing at lightning speed, industries everywhere are jumping on board. That's why diving into sales data is so crucial—it helps us spot market trends, find out what’s flying off the shelves, and understand what really drives sales. The goal of this project is to turn raw data into actionable insights that can fuel smarter marketing strategies and data-driven business moves.

Project Overview
This project digs into robotic sales data using SQL in Google BigQuery and brings the numbers to life with Looker Studio dashboards. The main focus? Uncovering overall sales health, mapping out customer buying habits, and identifying top-performing product categories.

Objectives
Check the pulse on overall sales performance.

Track down the ultimate best-selling robotic products.

Measure how much revenue comes in by quarter and year.

Decode customer purchasing habits and trends.

Build an interactive dashboard to make business decision-making a breeze.
